# Gallery softwares

## 1. The Brand Identity & Logo

The primary logo establishes the "Gallery" concept by framing a stylized upward trend (representing growth and value) within an elegant, structured border. It evokes a sense of curation, premium quality, and financial clarity.![[fin-app-suite.png]]
- **Logo Concept:** A framed upward arrow, visualizing "growth curated."
    
- **Palette:** Professional Sapphire Blue (#0056D6), Teal (#14B8A6), and an Accent Gold (#F59E0B).
    
- **Typography:** Clean, modern geometric sans-serif font (similar to Poppins or Inter).

## Gallery Finance Suite: Visual Identity Guidelines

### 1. Core Brand Identity
The "Gallery" brand focuses on the concept of curation, organization, and growth.

#### Palette:
* **Primary Sapphire:** `#0056D6`
* **Action Teal:** `#14B8A6`
* **Accent Gold:** `#F59E0B`
* **Neutral Text (Dark):** `#1E293B`
* **Background (Light):** `#F8FAFC`

---

### 2. Theme Definitions

#### Theme A: "Light Gallery" (Default)
Designed for clarity and daily focus.
* **Background:** `#FFFFFF`
* **Surface/Cards:** `#F8FAFC`
* **Primary Text:** `#1E293B`
* **Secondary Text:** `#64748B`
* **Border/Divider:** `#E2E8F0`

### Theme B: "Night Gallery" (Dark Mode)
Designed for immersive, professional analysis.
* **Background:** `#0F172A`
* **Surface/Cards:** `#1E293B`
* **Primary Text:** `#F8FAFC`
* **Secondary Text:** `#94A3B8`
* **Border/Divider:** `#334155`

---

### 3. Implementation Assets
The icons and logo utilize the Primary Sapphire and Action Teal as consistent anchors across both themes, while the Accent Gold provides functional highlights (e.g., status updates, "Goals").

#### Design Principles
1.  **Framing:** Use subtle borders to group related financial data.
2.  **Typography:** Use a clean, geometric sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins).
3.  **Color Hierarchy:** Sapphire for primary actions, Teal for secondary support/positive growth, Gold for key alerts/goals.

>**Design Tip for implementation:** When building your UI, ensure your primary brand colors—**Sapphire Blue (#0056D6)** and **Action Teal (#14B8A6)**—remain consistent across both themes. These act as your "anchor" colors. By keeping these stable, your app will retain its brand recognizability regardless of whether the user toggles to the Night theme. Use the **Accent Gold (#F59E0B)** specifically for functional highlights, like meeting a goal or flagging a critical transaction, to ensure it draws the eye effectively in both light and dark environments.
